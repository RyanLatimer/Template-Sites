Our Baking Philosophy

At Sunshine Bakery, we believe in the power of simple ingredients, time-honored techniques, and the magic that happens when communities gather around good food.

Every morning at 4am, our small team of dedicated bakers begins their day mixing, kneading, and shaping the doughs that will become your daily bread. We source our flour from local mills, our eggs from family farms, and our inspiration from the changing seasons.
